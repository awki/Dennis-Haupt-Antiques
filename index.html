About Us

We represent the American Craftsman furniture of Gustav Stickley, L. & J. G. Stickley, Charles Stickley, and Stickley Brothers, as well as Limbert, Lifetime, Roycroft, and other quality works from the best of the Arts and Crafts tradition.

In the fine arts area we have an excellent selection of period California paintings, drawings and prints.with particular focus on plen air impressionist works. We also carry sculpture including outstanding works in bronze both large and small.

In the decorative and applied arts areas we have articles of merit and quality particularly works reflecting the Arts and Crafts tradition including ceramics, rugs, textiles, and especially lighting.  We carry period desk and floor fixtures, architectural fixtures, and lampshades of both stained glass and mica.

Visit us online at dhantiques.com or visit our showroom at The Pasadena Antique Center located in historic Pasadena, California. Wherever you are, we are here to help find exactly what you need.
